Spaghetti

Description

Spaghetti with tomato sauce is a simple and classic Italian dish made from pasta tossed in a light, aromatic tomato and garlic sauce. It is quick to prepare, flavorful, and perfect for an everyday meal.

Ingredients

  • 200 g spaghetti
  • Salt
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic (finely chopped)
  • 400 g canned chopped tomatoes
  • Pepper
  • Fresh basil (optional)
  • Grated Parmesan (to serve)

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti in well-salted boiling water according to package instructions, then drain.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan, add garlic, and cook briefly until fragrant (do not brown).
  3. Add tomatoes, season with salt and pepper, and simmer for 10â€“15 minutes.
  4. Mix the sauce with the spaghetti and serve with basil and Parmesan.
